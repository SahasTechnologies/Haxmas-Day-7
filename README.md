## Haxmas tree with extra stuff! 

##### Features:
- Press decorate the tree and take it down to regenerate the baubles in different spots! 
- Press on any bauble to change it (in case you don't like it)
- press on the star ot the change star button on the bottom to change it to one of 6 stars in the repo. (if you have another star that you really love or ornament then remove background and pls pull request)
- There is a countdown to Christmas and the date in the top left
- IF it is december and before Christmas, then however many days are left, there will be those many baubles on the tree! _(for a visual representation of how many days left)_
